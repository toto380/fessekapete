Conditions

Program Rules

To ensure a clear and fair program, the following conditions apply:

  • The referral is validated only if the recommended client signs and becomes an active client.
  • Discounts apply from the next billing cycle after referral validation.
  • The referred client benefits from –5% on the first month only, for a 3-month minimum pack.
  • Offers cannot be combined with other discounts or promotions.
  • The program is reserved for Premium plans (3-month minimum packs).
  • StratAds reserves the right to modify or suspend the program at any time.
