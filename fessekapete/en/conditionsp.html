ARTICLE 1: PURPOSE AND DEFINITIONS

These General Terms and Conditions (hereinafter the "Terms") govern the consulting, tracking audit, Data Engineering (Server-Side), and maintenance services offered by StratAds (hereinafter "the Service Provider") to professional clients (hereinafter "the Client").

  • Audit: Investigation service of the Client's existing data collection infrastructure.
  • Implementation (Setup): One-time service for the creation, configuration, and deployment of a tracking architecture (Web and Server-Side).
  • Monitoring / Support: Recurring service for maintenance, data flow monitoring, and updates.
  • Data Leak: Any loss, non-transmission, or alteration of data between the Client's platforms and advertising networks.

ARTICLE 2: "ZERO DATA LEAK" GUARANTEE (Audit Specific)

The Service Provider offers an exclusive money-back guarantee on its initial Audit service. If, upon completion of its investigations, the Service Provider finds that the Client's tracking infrastructure is technically flawless and shows no data leaks whatsoever, the amount billed for the Audit will be fully refunded to the Client.

2.1 Guarantee Cancellation

The money-back guarantee is automatically voided, and the Audit service is considered fully justified and due, as soon as the Service Provider detects at least one of the following flaws:

  • Presence of alerts (e.g., "Red Alerts") or errors in the diagnostics of advertising networks (Google Ads, Meta Business Manager, etc.).
  • Event Match Quality (EMQ) score below 8/10 on the Meta interface.
  • Deduplication anomalies (duplicated events, absence of valid hash keys, missing Event IDs).
  • Server-Side (sGTM) configuration that is non-existent, defective, or blocked by AdBlockers / ITP due to improper DNS routing.
  • Absence or improper configuration of Google Consent Mode V2.
  • Any other technical flaw (visible via the F12 network console or Tag Assistants) resulting in a proven loss of First-Party Data.
