Identity & Vision

THE FIRM Our DNA

StratAds is not a marketing agency. We are a Data Engineering firm, built around an obsession: the absolute reliability of your tracking infrastructure.

The Focus: Zero Data Leaks

Where most agencies focus on creative production or Infrastructure Data, we look at the technical infrastructure. Our objective is clear: to secure the transmission of your ad data (Server-Side, CAPI) to feed algorithms without loss.

Every data loss represents an amputated ROI. Guaranteeing exact conversion tracking and integrating Consent Mode V2 is what distinguishes a basic setup from a premium infrastructure built to last.

We don't manage your campaigns. We build the pipelines and technical foundations of your web ecosystem. Our engineers protect your ad revenues from structural obsolescence (AdBlockers, ITP, GDPR).
